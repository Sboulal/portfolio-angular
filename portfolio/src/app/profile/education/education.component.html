School 1337
  • 1337 is Morocco's first free coding school, established in 2018. It provides completely free IT training, open 24/7 to anyone aged 18-30 with no prior programming experience required. 1337 Coding School The school uses a unique peer-learning approach where students learn through collaborative projects rather than traditional classes. It currently has campuses in Khouribga and Ben Guérir with over 500 students. 1337: Morocco's First Coding School The name "1337" translates to "LEET" (elite) in coding language, reflecting its mission to train Morocco's next generation of elite programmers.

institut de gestion et d'informatique ofppt khouribga
  • The Institut Spécialisé de Gestion et d'Informatique (ISGI) in Khouribga is part of Morocco's OFPPT system (Office de la Formation Professionnelle et de Promotion du Travail). Located in Hay Rachad, Khouribga, it specializes in management and information technology training. Institut Spécialisé de Gestion et d'Informatique - Khouribga | OFPPT As an OFPPT institution, it offers professional vocational training programs that combine theoretical coursework with practical applications, preparing students for careers in business management and IT sectors. The institute provides diploma programs, qualifying courses, and evening classes, making technical education accessible to students at various levels and schedules.
